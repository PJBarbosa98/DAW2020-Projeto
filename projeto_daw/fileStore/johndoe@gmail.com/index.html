A Place For Cats

Made By Paulo Barbosa

According to the Cambridge Dictionary of the English language, cats are small animals with fur, four legs, a tail, and claws, usually kept as pets. Nonetheless, all cat owners know they are much more than that. They are their owners. Nobody can boss them around!

Around ten thousand years ago, cats decided -- on their own -- to be domesticated [1]. Since then, they have established a good bond with humans. In fact, felines are among the most popular pets in the world today, with as many as 74 million cats living in U.S. homes. And that is not for no reason! There are many benefits of owning a cat. Stay here to know a little more about these amazing creatures.

Cats are featured in the history of many nations, are the subject of legend, and are a favorite subject of artists and writers. Considered to be sacred animals, in Egypt, they were associated with the goddesses Isis and Bastet. It is known that "killing a cat was forbidden and the Greek historian Herodotus reports that, whenever a household cat died, the entire family would mourn and shave their eyebrows." (see [2]).

Coming right up, a few curiosities regarding cats will be presented.

Diversity

According to The International Cat Association (TICA), as of last year, 71 cat breeds are recognized. Each of these is and behaves differently towards their owners. So, next time you are deciding on getting a cat, make sure you pick the right one for you! Check [3] for a detailed list of cat breeds along with their description.

Benefits

There are many benefits to owning a cat! They will help you cope with painful situations, find a significant other, lower your stress level, improve your sleep, et cetera (for more, see [4]). As stated before, it is no coincidence they are the most popular choice for a pet.

Curiosities

Did you know cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping? Did you know the wealthiest cat in the world has a fortune of over 7 million euros? Did you know Sir Isaac Newton invented the cat door? There are many fun facts about cats you probably did not know about. If you are curious, check [5]! I am sure you will have a fun time reading the article.
